Meet Cody — Chef & Founder

With more than 10 years of culinary experience, Cody brings creativity, precision, and a passion for exceptional food to every table. Having trained under Michelin-starred chefs and worked in Michelin-starred restaurants, Cody has developed a refined approach to fine dining while embracing the artistry of innovative, ingredient-driven cuisine.

Experienced in contemporary and diversified cuisine, Cody is also an expert in fermented foods, including kefir and kombucha, incorporating both flavor and wellness into thoughtfully crafted menus. Known for pushing culinary boundaries, Cody enjoys creating original dishes by pairing unexpected ingredients into harmonious and memorable dining experiences.

Whether preparing an elegant fine dining menu, cooking over an open fire outdoors, or providing personalized in-home chef services, Cody is dedicated to delivering unforgettable meals tailored to each client's vision. With extensive experience catering intimate gatherings as well as large events, every menu is designed with care, creativity, and the highest standards of quality.

For Cody, cooking is more than a profession—it's a lifelong passion. Every dish tells a story, celebrates fresh ingredients, and invites guests to experience familiar flavors in exciting new ways.

Our Philosophy

Fire. Earth. Microbes. Time.

Four elemental forces shape everything we create, teach, and share.

At Earth Doctors, we do not "prepare food." We cultivate living systems. We believe fermentation is not mere preservation — it is biological intelligence guided with precision. The microbiome is not a wellness trend — it is the foundational ecology of human clarity, mood regulation, immune function, and longevity.

Fire

The Primordial Kitchen

In the forests beneath Mount Shasta, we build our kitchens from earth and stone. Rocket stoves. Earthen ovens. Live-fire grills. From these elements, cuisine emerges — slow, intentional, microbial, precise. Fire is not decoration. Fire is transformation. It changes mineral bioavailability, activates enzymes, and creates flavor compounds no oven can replicate.

Earth

Before Fermentation, Soil

The microbial landscape of our gut mirrors the microbial landscape of living soil. When we destroy soil biology through industrial agriculture, we destroy the very template our bodies need for digestive intelligence. At Earth Doctors, we begin every protocol with the ground itself — regenerative soil systems that produce nutrient-dense, microbially rich ingredients.

Microbes

Cultivating Trillions of Allies

Fermentation is the oldest biotechnology on earth — and the most sophisticated. Through koji cultivation, lacto-fermentation, and extended aging, we guide microbial communities to create foods that don't just taste extraordinary, but actively support the gut-brain axis, immune modulation, and neurochemical balance.

Time

The Silent Architect

An 18-month miso cannot be rushed. A properly developed koji culture requires precise temperature windows measured in hours. Time is the invisible ingredient in every great ferment — the patience to allow microbial intelligence to fully express itself. We honor this by never cutting corners and never compromising on aging protocols.

The Commitment

This is Not Rustic Homesteading.
This is Elemental Fine Dining.

Everything we create — from live-fire tasting menus to aged miso — is designed to restore digestive coherence and awaken sensory awareness. We bridge the gap between ancestral wisdom and modern microbiome science, between the sacred kitchen and the laboratory.
